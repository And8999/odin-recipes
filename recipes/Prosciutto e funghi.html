Prosciutto e funghi

Description

Prosciutto e funghi is a pizza variety that is topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, thin slices of prosciutto

cotto, and mushrooms. Some varieties can be topped with olives served drizzled with olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Prosciutto cottoMushrooms
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Prepare the oven
  2. Prepare the pizza sauce
  3. cut the mozzarella
  4. Add ketchup
